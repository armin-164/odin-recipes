Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This is a grilled sandwich.

It is quite delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 15g salted butter
  • 2 slices of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Spread the butter evenly on two slices of bread
  2. Add the cheese slices
  3. Add it on a pan or use a grill
