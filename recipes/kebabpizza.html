Kebabpizza

Description

This is a kebabpizza.

It is the best pizza without a doubt

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Döner Kebab
  • Onions
  • Fefferoni
  • Garlic sauce

Steps

  1. Shape the dough with a rolling pin
  2. Add the tomato sauce
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Add the Döner kebab
  5. Add the Onions and fefferoni
  6. Put it in the oven at 200C
  7. After 20 minutes you can take it out and then add the garlic sauce when it has cooled a bit.
